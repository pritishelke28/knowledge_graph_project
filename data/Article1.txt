Tesla, Inc. is a pioneering American multinational automotive and clean energy company that has transformed the global transport industry. The visionary billionaire Elon Musk serves as the chief executive officer (CEO) of Tesla and manages its daily strategic growth. While originally founded in California, Tesla officially moved its corporate headquarters to Austin, Texas to accommodate massive new manufacturing facilities. 

The company specializes in designing and manufacturing advanced electric vehicles, residential-to-grid battery energy storage systems, and solar roofing products. To rapidly accelerate its transition to sustainable energy, Tesla acquired SolarCity in 2016 for 2.6 billion dollars, merging solar production directly with its battery storage division. Today, Tesla operates multiple massive Gigafactories worldwide to meet the surging global demand for clean energy transportation.
